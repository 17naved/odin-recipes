Biryani

About Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice. For my Biryani, I simplify the order of operations, while retaining the traditional layered approach to assembling it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Aromatics
  • Herbs
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Ghee
  • Chilli Powder
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken the herbs and chilli powder
  2. Add Ghee and onions and saute the onions until caramelized
  3. Prepare the rice
  4. Add chciken in caramalized onions and fry until browned
  5. Top the Chicken in the pot on the rice in a layered form
  6. Add more caramelized onions and saffron in the pot with the rice in layer
  7. Transfer the Chicken Biryani to the servign platter
